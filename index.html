Bear Gang Entertainment

The best young recording and publishing label company, founded in 2020. based in the township of Palm Ridge in the City of Ekurhuleni District. We find pride and culture in the township life as it embraces the epitome of the future world, where different races and culture can live in unity, compassion and trust. Due to this our music embrace and embody the lifestyle and culture within our Kasi'.

With a catalog of young talented and bred from the ghetto artists, we are able to share the life style and voice out our Kasi culture. We envision a culturally and economically thriving township economy, and we take our position as the hub of the next generation of shinning talent. Music, culture, dance, and poetry are all sources of language and experience, our goal is to be the best publishing and talent management company.
